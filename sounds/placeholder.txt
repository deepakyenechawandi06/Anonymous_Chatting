This folder contains sound effects for emoji animations.

Required sound files:
- whoosh.mp3 (for wave emoji)
- pop.mp3 (for thumbs up emoji)
- thud.mp3 (for thumbs down emoji)
- clap.mp3 (for clap emoji)
- sizzle.mp3 (for fire emoji)
- heartbeat.mp3 (for heart emoji)
- laugh.mp3 (for laugh emoji)
- party.mp3 (for party emoji)

Place the sound files in this directory for the emoji animations to play sounds.
